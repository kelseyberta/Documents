    W02 Course Outcomes Assessment

    • Due Wednesday by 11:59pm
    • Points 10
    • Submitting a file upload

    Overview

    • Task: Assess yourself on the course outcomes.
    • Purpose: To demonstrate your level of understanding with this course.

    Instructions

    1. Download the Course Outcomes Assessment. Download Course Outcomes Assessment.
      Minimize File Preview
    2. Review the outcomes, marking the columns indicating the level of your current skills, then write a brief paragraph or two about what you think of the outcomes. Include which outcomes you don't understand or which ones you think you may have the most trouble with.
    3. Submit this assignment.
